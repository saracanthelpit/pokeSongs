Song Converter

Each mat can face any of four directions, giving access to the full chromatic scale across two octaves. Nearly any song can be played.

↑
North — Natural note C4 through B4 (Low Do → Ti)
←
West — Sharp (+½ step) C♯ D♯ F♯ G♯ A♯ (the five black keys)
↓
South — High octave D5 through B5 (Re′ → Ti′)
→
East — Flat (−½ step) Same pitches as West, approached from above
